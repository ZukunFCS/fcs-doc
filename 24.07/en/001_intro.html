What is FCS?

FCS is a new facial capture system that adoped algorithms cultivated by digital human research (DHP research). Equipped with an auto-tracking function, it shortens the time required for facial tracking and reduces the burden of the user. In this instruction manual, you can learn about FCS and how it works.

With FCS, you can process videos taken offline.

It reads video data, and can also transfer the analyzed animation into 3D characters.

Help and support

If you have any questions about how to use FCS, please contact us from the HELTEC website (https://sensing.heltec.co.jp/contact/).
